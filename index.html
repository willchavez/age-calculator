What this age calculator does

This free age calculator helps you find your exact age from a start date (often a date of birth). It shows your age in years, months, and days, and is designed to be simple, fast, and private.

How age is calculated

Age is calculated by comparing your selected date to today’s date, accounting for different month lengths and leap years.
